Brand Overview

AmeriServ is a full-service print and promotional products company dedicated to helping businesses, organizations, and families communicate and celebrate with quality printing, exceptional service, and small-town care. Proudly serving Hartsville and the surrounding community since 1977, we believe every printed piece carries a story — and we’re honored to help bring those stories to life.

Mission

To deliver high-quality printing and promotional products with personal service and community-focused care, helping our customers share their message with impact.

Vision

To be the most trusted print partner in the region, known for craftsmanship, reliability, and relationships built on service and integrity.

Brand Promise

Your small-town partner for big-time printed impact.

Whether it’s a first business card, church tithing envelopes, a wedding invitation suite, or a custom sign for Main Street — AmeriServ shows up with the same level of care, craft, and commitment.

Values & Personality

AmeriServ is like the neighbor everyone trusts with their important projects — warm, steady, and proud of a job well done. Our values and voice guide how we design, write, and serve.

Brand Values

  • Craftsmanship – Every detail matters; quality you can see and feel.
  • Service – Real people, real support, real care.
  • Community – Supporting local businesses, churches, schools, and families.
  • Reliability – Dependable turnaround and consistent results.

Brand Traits

  • • Warm & neighborly
  • • Professional & dependable
  • • Community-focused
  • • Helpful & solution-oriented

Voice Guidelines

  • • Speak like a real person, not a script.
  • • Focus on the customer’s event, goal, or milestone.
  • • Be clear, encouraging, and straightforward.
  • • Avoid jargon and overly “salesy” language.

Sounds like AmeriServ

Not our style

“Act now! Limited-time print offer you can’t afford to miss!!!”

03

Logos & Usage

The AmeriServ logo is the primary expression of our brand. It should always appear clear, legible, and uncompromised. Use only the approved artwork files provided in the logo package.

Clear Space

Leave at least one “A” width of space around the logo on all sides. This protects legibility and impact, especially at smaller sizes.

Minimum Size

Print: 1.25" wide · Digital: 150px wide

Do / Don’t

Do

  • • Use approved logo files
  • • Keep colors true to the palette
  • • Maintain clear space
  • • Place on solid, high-contrast backgrounds

Don’t

  • • Don’t stretch or squash
  • • Don’t recolor arbitrarily
  • • Don’t add drop shadows or outlines
  • • Don’t place over busy images

File Formats

Logos are provided as SVG and PNG for digital use, and AI/EPS for print production. Always start from vector files when sending artwork to other printers or vendors.

Color System

AmeriServ’s palette is built around a modern Americana feel — deep navy, heritage red, warm cream, and grounded charcoal. Use these consistently for brand recognition across print and digital.

AmeriServ Navy

HEX: #0A2342

RGB: 10, 35, 66

Use: Backgrounds, headings

AmeriServ Red

HEX: #B22222

RGB: 178, 34, 34

Use: Buttons, accents

Cream

HEX: #F3EDE3

RGB: 243, 237, 227

Use: Backgrounds, light text areas

Charcoal

HEX: #2E2E2E

RGB: 46, 46, 46

Use: Body text, contrast

Accessibility & Contrast

When placing text over color, ensure sufficient contrast. Use cream or white text on navy and red; use charcoal text on cream or very light backgrounds. Avoid red-on-navy and navy-on-red for body copy.

05

Typography System

AmeriServ’s typography should feel clear, confident, and approachable. Gotham is the hero typeface used in the logo and key headings, supported by clean, legible system fonts for digital use.

Primary Typeface

Gotham – used for logo, major headings, and key brand statements.

  • • Gotham Bold — Headlines & hero statements
  • • Gotham Medium — Subheads & callouts
  • • Gotham Book — Body copy in print layouts

Digital Fallbacks

On the web, when Gotham isn’t available, use:

  • • Headings: system-ui (platform default sans-serif)
  • • Body: system-ui or Arial

Type Scale (Suggested)

  • Hero / H1: 48–60pt (print) / 2.5–3rem (web)
  • H2: 32–40pt / 2–2.5rem
  • H3: 24–30pt / 1.5–1.875rem
  • Body: 14–16pt / ~1rem
  • Small / Captions: 11–12pt / 0.75rem

Keep line heights generous for readability, especially in longer blocks of text or on cream backgrounds.

06

Visual Style & Applications

Our visuals should feel handcrafted, real, and rooted in the community. We highlight the people, processes, and pieces that make AmeriServ special.

Photography

  • • Real shop scenes, presses, and hands at work
  • • Local businesses, churches, schools, and events
  • • Natural, warm light over harsh flash
  • • Focus on details: ink on paper, foil, texture

Graphic Elements

  • • Soft circular motifs (echoing service icons on the site)
  • • Thin cream dividers at ~50% width for section breaks
  • • Subtle paper or linen textures in backgrounds
  • • Minimal ornament — let the work speak

Common Applications

  • • Business cards & stationery systems
  • • Multi-part forms & envelopes
  • • Brochures, booklets, and flyers
  • • Signs, banners, and window graphics
  • • Promotional products & branded merch
  • • Wedding & event suites
